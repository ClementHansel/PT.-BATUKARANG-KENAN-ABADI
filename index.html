About Us

Founded on march 2024, PT bkka is a company that focus on iot solution. To provide the industries needs that growing ever complex. We are dedicated to help organizations and institution to achieve their best version with efficiency and security through integrated smart technology.

Vision

  1. Empowering Indonesia and the world with transformative AI and software solutions, fostering innovation, and shaping a future where technology bridges boundaries and uplifts communities. Through cutting-edge research and collaboration, we aim to redefine excellence in the tech industry and contribute to global academic and technological advancement
  2. To pioneer the creation of generational high technology that accelerates Indonesia's technological advancement, empowering industries, communities, and institutions to achieve global competitiveness and long-term sustainability
  3. To normalize advanced technology by making it affordable, accessible, and adaptable, ensuring its benefits reach every individual and community, driving equitable progress and fostering innovation at all levels

Mission

  1. To lead in AI and software innovation by delivering world-class solutions that address global challenges, while actively contributing to academic research and knowledge-sharing. We strive to build a collaborative ecosystem that fosters learning, nurtures talent, and transforms technological potential into impactful advancements for Indonesia and the world
  2. To design and deliver cutting-edge technological solutions that address Indonesia's critical challenges, empower local innovation, and establish a sustainable foundation for long-term global competitiveness. We are committed to fostering partnerships, driving research, and investing in talent development to create transformative technologies that serve current and future generations
  3. To develop and distribute advanced technologies that are affordable, user-friendly, and easily adoptable by individuals and organizations at all levels. By prioritizing inclusivity and innovation, we aim to bridge the digital divide, empower communities, and enable equitable access to technology that drives progress and prosperity
